1. What do I think ML learns from data?
Answer: ML learns statistical patterns that consistently reduce prediction error, not real-world causes.

2. What do I think EDA is used for?
Answer: EDA helps me understand what the data is capable of telling the model — and what it will never tell it.

3. What do I think a “good model” means?
Answer: A good model is one that generalizes well, is stable under small data changes, and fails in understandable ways.
